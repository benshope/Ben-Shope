UP Brain Trainer

This is a cross-platform app that I wrote with AngularJS and Cordova. My goal was to create a friendly space to perform N-Back brain training without the pattern-recognition games in mainstream brain-training apps.

App Engine CMS

I want a better way to build websites. Not PHP-based like Wordpress, not limited like Tumblr, and not closed-source like Squarespace. This project is under construction and available on Github.

Evolution Simulation

This is an artificial life simulation and genetic algorithm that I wrote in OpenCL. The program is designed to scale efficiently - to get meaningful results from current, and near-future, hardware.

Window Pods

I love indoor gardening - and I wanted to fill my entire window with plants to take full advantage of the sunlight. There wasn't a window planter on the market - so I made one. It finished on KickStarter and is moving to retail.

Deep Map

This is an app designed to visualize global map data. Deep Map enables users to visually overlay global data sets to gain insights and make connections. I am currently building-out the social maps.

Autispeak

This is a cross-platform app that helps kids with Autism to learn and communicate. Autispeak has a fluid layout and turns any mobile device into a tool for AAC (augmentative and alternative communication.)
